# Sales Offer

## One-sentence value proposition

Get a focused, buyer-first teardown of your B2B SaaS homepage in 48 hours so you can fix unclear messaging, weak CTAs, missing proof, and demo-flow friction before sending more traffic.

## Buyer persona

Founder, product marketer, or growth lead at an early-stage B2B SaaS / AI tool with a live homepage and a demo/signup CTA.

## Trigger moment

- Recent launch or upcoming Product Hunt / directory launch.
- Page gets traffic but demo/signup conversion is weak or unknown.
- Founder suspects messaging is too generic.
- Paid ads or outbound campaign is about to start.

## Price anchor

- Founding slot: **$199** for a 48-hour written teardown.
- Later standard: **$399** with async walkthrough.

## Guarantee/refund boundary

If the buyer says the teardown has no actionable recommendations, redo once or refund. No guaranteed conversion, revenue, ranking, compliance, fundraising, or investment outcomes.

## Fastest first-dollar path

Cold email a precise observation, ask for a reply with `teardown` or `sample`, confirm fit, then create manual Stripe/invoice/payment link for the $199 slot.
